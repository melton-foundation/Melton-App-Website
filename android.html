THE MELTON FOUNDATION APP FOR ANDROID

The melton foundation app is a one stop solution for all your MF related needs.
Find below the steps to download the app on your Android device.

STEPS TO DOWNLOAD THE APP

Below are the steps to download The Melton Foundation App on your Android device.
The download link is given below

STEP 1

Select the 'Download for Android' button present at the bottom of this screen.

STEP 2

Your device may issue a warning message. Kindly ignore the message as the app is a 100% safe
Select the 'OK' option on your device.

STEP 3

Once the file has downloaded on your device, it will show an option to 'open'
click on the 'open' option to install the application

STEP 4

Once you select the 'open' button, your device may show a message as shown above. Kindly select the 'INSTALL' option to begin the installation of the application on your device.

STEP 5

The installation process may take a few seconds. While the above screen is displayed, do not go back or switch off your device

STEP 6

Once the installation is done, your device will display the message 'App Installed' and the app will be visible on your device.
To open the App immidiately, select the 'open' option and to open it later on, you may open it directly from your device!
